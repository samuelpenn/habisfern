Brief Timeline of the East

The Coming of the Ice

As the Ice flowed down out of the Spines of Ice, east and west were effectively cut off from each other. From the north, the unstoppable advance of the Ice wiped all trace of human civilisation out of its path.

Leading the advance of the Ice were those creatures in thrall to it - the giants led by Snaw, the spirits and wraiths together with their posessed, and the inhuman men who had fallen under the domination of the North. Weakened by famine, and plagued by disease, mankind was no match for these foes, and the brief and terrible wars which preceeded the Ice's advance caused untold loss of life and suffering.

The survivors flee southward, to the warm grasslands still unaffected by the encroaching cold. There though they find only war again, this time between the city states of men.

Out of the chaos is born Yorael, son of Hraldin, who is gifted with the voice of leadership and a mind crafted for war. Rising to become King of Urbrannen, he subjugates the nations around him, until a peace of sorts is reached. Taking an army northwards, he comes to the Castle of Snaw, built on the Ice a league above the buried city of Ubra. There, Snaw is defeated, and the god Rorth is freed from the clutches of the Ice.

After the Ice

Defeated, and lacking leadership, the forces of the Ice fall to bickering, and halt their advance southward. As the Ice slowly begins its march back Northwards, so the city of Urbrannen.

1

Yorael becomes King of Urbrannen. Formation of a new, stable, human kingdom based at Urbrannen. The defeat of Snaw, and the beginnings of the retreat of the Ice.

200

The priests of Hraldin take control of the Kingdom of Urbrannen, forming the Hraldic Theocracy. All other gods are outlawed.

1000

The Ice retreats up into the Spines of Ice, clearing all lands south of this range of mountains. Northern plains are still glaciated, though the Ice is rapidly retreating. The plains around Urbrannen are becoming marshy, and to the far east, swampland is becoming common.
