Encyclopedia of Habisfern

Habisfern is a fantasy campaign world. It is low fantasy, with realistic ecologies, histories and societies. Magic exists, and is relatively common, but low powered.

The purpose of this encyclopedia is to describe all facets of this world, in an easily accessible form. It is currently limited in the number of entries, but is growing as older documents are converted into the encyclopedia format.

It is currently very much a work in progress. Most of the pages here are auto generated, so expect the format of the encyclopedia to change radically at any time as the technology behind the pages is updated and modified.
