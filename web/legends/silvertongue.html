Silver Tongue and Heart

A set consisting of a sword and shield, forged by the Mage Smith Gern of Teal in 1182, for Renbric, Slayer of the White Wyrm. When they were first made, a scabbard set with jewels was included, though this was not known to have any special properties. It has since been lost.

Renbric was slain by the Necromancer Halgorn at Rorn's Vale. Halgorn was in the employ of Renbric, but he betrayed him, and stole his money, later returning with an undead army in order to wipe out evidence of his crimes. Halgorn was initially defeated by Renbric's son, Guthred, but later the entire family was murdered, and the bodies of Guthred and his sister Gute were stolen.

Silver Tongue

The sword was lost at the battle of Rorn's Vale, but was later found to have fallen into the river, and was picked up by a warrior in the area some years later.

Silver Heart

This was taken with the body of Guthred, and is assumed lost.
