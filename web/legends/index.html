Rune Magic
Rune magic is a form of magic used by Mage Smiths, who are able to instill virtues into items - normally weapons and armour. The type of virtue a weapon has can be read from the runes carved into it. A guide to runes is available.

Legends and Lore

Legendary Weapons and Armour

  • Persecutor.
  • Rim's Truth, a Necromancer's sword.
  • Silver Tongue, a bright silver sword, paired with a matching shield called Silver Heart.
