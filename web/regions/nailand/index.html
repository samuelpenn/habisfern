What the Kingdom Says
Nailand is a barbaric land, inhabited by the barely human Short Folk who raid the borders of the Kingdom on a regular basis. Eventually, Nailand will be conquered, and its inhabitants either killed or subsumed into civilised society.
What Midlaen Says
Nailand is a barbaric land, inhabited by the barely human Short Folk who raid our borders on a regular basis. They also raid the Kingdom of Rorn however, which keeps both groups occupied - often enough for both of them to leave us alone.

Nailand

Made up of lightly forested plains, hills and mountains, Nailand is home to the Short Folk, one of the pre-human inhabiteds of the Isle. The Short Folk are a tribal people, averaging 150cm in height, and being generally dark, hairy and having little understanding of personal hygiene.
