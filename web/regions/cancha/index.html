What Tanwall Says
Cancha considers itself civilised, and looks down on our Cults as being barbaric and inhuman. They don't realise the gifts they threw away. If they continue to fight us, then the Animal Gods will lead us in killing their men, and taking their women for our own.
What Danuch Says
They are a proud people, but they don't know who they are. They have no gods of their own, so they borrow from others as it suits them. Sometimes they borrow from us. Our Druids go amongst them, and teach them the lore the Mother. In time, the goddess will adopt them.

Cancha
