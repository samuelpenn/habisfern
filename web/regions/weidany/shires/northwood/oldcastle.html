Who Lives Here
A small group of outlaws currently have residence here. They are left alone by the Kingdom, since there are far worse things living in the Troll Watch than this lot, who mostly keep to themselves.

Who Rules
Penda the Mouse is in charge. A small, wiry man who's appearance gave him his name. He is a competent leader however, as well as being a dangerous killer and a priest of Tharos.
