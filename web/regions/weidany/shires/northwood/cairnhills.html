Who Lives Here
Around the edges of the hills can be found several human villages, the largest of which is Rorn's Vale. The central portions of the hills have no permanent inhabitants, and is declared to be forest land by the Queen.

Who Rules
The hills come under the authority of the hundred of Rorn's Vale, though for the most part the law is considered unenforceable up on the hills themselves. Unless something is causing trouble for the villages nearby, anything up on the hills is left free to do as it pleases.

Rorn's Vale

Rorn's Vale is a typical hundred town, with a population of about four hundred.

The Cairns

Spread across the southern hills in a rough east - west line are the cairns. There are about four dozen in all, though legend has it that their true number cannot be accurately counted. There are six major cairns, which each have extensive crypts beneath them. The rest are single caves, or simple mounds containing no more than one or two bodies.
