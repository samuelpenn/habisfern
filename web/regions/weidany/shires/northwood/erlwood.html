Who Rules

Thegn Redwald, House of Oswald

Redwald is a 52 year old man, an aging warrior who last fought in the wars against Cadwold over a decade ago. He has not aged well. He is however friendly, outspoken and still intellectually active.

His wife is Heldith, a 51 year old woman with still black hair.
