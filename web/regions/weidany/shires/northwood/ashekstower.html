Who Lives Here
The inhabitants here are not fully human. Ghouls, some trolls and other, less well known, inhabitants have been seen here. It is said that Elders live in the deep depths.

Who Rules
Unknown. Anarchy and madness rules this place, and there are no known leaders amongst the inhabitants.
