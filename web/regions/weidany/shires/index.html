Hundreds
Hundreds are administrative divisions of land, and have no relationship to the ownership of land by various nobles.

Hides
A hide is roughly 120 acres in size.

Shires of the Kingdom of Rorn

The Kingdom is split into twenty shires, mostly for administrative purposes. Each shire has a shire-reeve who has the job of collecting taxes, and ensuring the the Queen's justice is carried out where necessary.

Each shire is divided into hundreds - each roughly a hundred hides (about twenty villages) in size.
