The 9th Century

801

Some in South Danuch fall under the power of the Druids, and there is fighting against them by their brethren.

814

Scarandy raids into Merkia, but is driven back when the morning after a great battle the dead rise up and ?ght against them.

833

A mighty force raids into Emrend from the south eastern lands. It has men and some of the short folk amongst it, and practises much magic. It does not stay.

836

The Force comes again to Emrend, taking half of it despite much fighting, and settling there over the winter. The King of Emrend sends for aid to his neighbours.

837

The Force is met by armies of Ornwold and Talmoth. They are defeated, and the Force exacts a high price to stay out of Ornwold. It asks a similar price the following year.

839

Ornwold pays a great price to the Force to keep the peace, but the Force goes west anyway, and Ornwold falls to the Force.

There is fighting between the Force and the men of Westland.

840

Oswine son of Penda son of Cenwahl, King of Perrin takes a thousand men to meet the Force, bringing with him two wizards of much madness. They meet the Force at Fendyke in Ornwold, where the Force is within a fort.

The wizards bring down the walls of the fort, and battle is met. Many die, but there is no clear victor. The Force retreats back across Ornwold, meeting up with others until there is a second battle at Edbismarsh. The Force is broken, and Yees east to Emrend. Oswine cannot follow, and goes back to Perrin.

841

Many warrior women of Estwed who follow Asabeth lead raids against the Force. It asks for peace, and is granted it. The Force settles in Emrend, but gives up much gold.

843

After several years of fighting along the border, Wulfheard, son of Aethelheard, King of North Lanuch leads an invasion into Western Lanuch, slaying Aelle son of Edwin, King of Western Lanuch. Wulfheard claims the Western Kingdom as his own.

880

Cult of Women of Estwed lead raids into Aiden, and much land is taken.

885

Queen Osith leads Estwed against East Lanuch, taking much land and cattle.

889

Queen Osith of Estwed is killed by treachery by the men of her Kingdom. The Cult of Asabeth is wiped out. Peada becomes King of Estwed by right of force.
