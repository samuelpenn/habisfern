What Herzan Says
The shackles of Neimstarg were thrown off less than two hundred years ago. If they try again to force their will upon us, we will kill them, and their wives and children until there are no more of them left to try.
What Urazen Says
Neimstarg is a civilised kingdom, good for trade, and an excellent buffer against the fanatics in Herzan. Their mercenaries are also useful against Trollund where necessary.

The Kingdom of Niemstarg

The Kingdom of Niemstarg is a rich trading kingdom on the north coast of the island. It has a small but highly trained army which it hires out to its neighbours for a huge profit.
