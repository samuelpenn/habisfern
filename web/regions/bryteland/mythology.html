Mythology of Bryteland

There are two main religions in Bryteland, the principle one being that of Voltan and his followers. Voltan is a sky god, a warrior and a noble who is see as the hero and protector of the Brytish people. Voltan is almost identical to Haltan in the Rornic pantheon, and the two mythologies are very similar. Each of the Brytish gods has a parallel with a Rornic god, though in some cases several Rornic gods have been subsumed into a single god. The Brytish seem to prefer simplicity.

The second religion is the Cult of Ashek, which is gaining a foothold in Bryteland.
