What the Kingdom Says
The Hruul Forest is an uninhabited wilderness of thick, magical forests.
What Neimstarg Says
The Hruul Forest is a dangerous wilderness, inhabited by the descendants of the Milindor Empire that ruled there over seven hundred years ago. The people that dwell there are inhuman, and to be avoided as much as possible.

The Hruul Forest

The central parts of Euressa are blanketed with thick forest, considered impassable by many of the surrounding nations. It is the home to the fey - those beings which are not quite human, who live on the borders between this world and the Otherside.
