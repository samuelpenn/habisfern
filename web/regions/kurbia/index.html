Kingdom of Kurbia

The coastal kingdom of Kurbia lays east of the Ice Spine Mountains, near the north-east corner of Euressa. It is bordered in the north by mountains and sea, to the west by the False Mountains and Pavnik. South lays the Kingdom of Ranistan, with which Kurbia is often at war.
