Belogan Mythology

There was the universe, and it was called Beloga. The universe created the Three Mothers, who were Ladma, Zharva and Dalvi. The mated with themselves, and produced Morav, who was Death, and Svargi who was to be the Father. Svargi mated with Ladma and Zharva, and together they begat the Earth. Svargi then mated with Morav, and so were born the first humans. Some of these rose to become gods, while others stayed in the world.

Soon after this, there was much war amongst the gods. They bickered over the mortals and each claimed as many as they could as woshippers. Each god wanted domain over the Earth, so there was much fighting. Many gods died, most of whom are now forgotton.

Then there was the great war against the Ice, when the Great Winter settled upon the Earth, and most upon it died. Only after Svargi united the remaining gods against it was it defeated. Shortly after the defeat of the Ice, there was peace amongst the gods, but Svargi could see that the peace was a fragile thing that could not last.

So Svargi went to Beloga, and after many quests gained wisdom from the universe. Makosh was created, who was Fate. She gave to Svargi the Secret Wisdoms, and told him to distribute it amongst the gods, for the Wisdoms would bind the gods to a particular aspect of the Universe, and they would have control over that aspect and none other. That way the gods would be restricted from fighting over the same things.

The gods were persuaded into accepting this idea, mainly because Svargi held all the Wisdom, which gave him ultimate power. The gods gamed for the Secret Wisdoms, and it was dealt out as they one it.
