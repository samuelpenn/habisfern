The Elders
The Elders were a race of inhuman sorcerers who lived on the Isle since before the last Ice Age. They were destroyed with the arrival of Man, but many of their artifacts and places of worship still remain.

The Druids

The Druids held absolute power in Weidany before the time the Rornic people call the Betrayal. After this time, the Rornic peoples migrated northwards, into Weidany, displacing the Weidanic cultures which lived there. The power of the Druids was broken, and now it onyl enjoys the same levels of power in Danuch.

The Druids base their worship around the Earth Mother Yammath, who is a symbol of both death and creation. Much of their original power came also from Elder magic.
