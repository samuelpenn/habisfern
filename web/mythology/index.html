Who is Right?
There are many pantheons of gods, and many of the mythologies contradict each other in a variety of ways. The mythologies are the beliefs of the people. Though belief does not define reality, it does affect how people live their lives, and interpret divine signs.

Mythologies

Mythologies describe the world - both what it is, and how it got there. They are a mixture of truth, fiction and moral viewpoint. As such, there are many different mythologies to be found across the world, some more incompatible than others.

In some cases, the differences in two mythologies is no more than a shift in names, a different emphasis on the stories, and a change in the view point. In others, two very different mythologies have shared gods, or stories telling of meetings between each pantheon.
