Mythology of Bryteland

The Brytish god are similar in aspect to those of the Rornic people, though there are quite a few differences. Influence comes from the Weidanic and Rornic mythologies, as well as those of Tanwall and Cancha.

The Gods

    Voltan, King of the Sky. He is the great sky god, who stands for war and victory over adversity. He is also the god of the weather and the sea, including sailing and navigation. He could be considered another aspect of the Rornic Haltan.
    Freeja, Goddess of the Moon. The consort of Voltan, Freeja is the goddess of women and the home, of certainty, oaths, marriage and childbirth.
    Rim, Guardian of the Dead.
    Jorda, Mother of the Earth. She is the Earth goddess, who mated with Iss to give birth to Jortooth, the World Dragon.
