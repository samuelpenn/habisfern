Interests
Gyltith is the prophet, who is cursed only to see the futures she does not wish to see. She is goddess of sorrow and remorse, also of fear and emotional suffering.
Worshippers
No-one wants the eyes of Gyltith to pass over them, for if they do, then they suffer a foreshadowing of their own misfortune. Warriors who know they are going to their deaths often drink to Gyltith, for she understands what they face.

Gyltith, the Sorrowful

Gyltith was born of Rorn and Estror, and like her sister Hreth, fate did not turn out well for her. At first, she sought knowledge of the world, and aimed to write down all that had happened in the world.

She grew tired though of always being behind in what happened, and tried to find an easy way to keep up to date, by knowing what was going to happen before it did. She believed that the future of the world was foretold in the Dreams of Iss, and when Yagma and Rim refused to tell her of them, she travelled to the far north, to the great castle of the frost giant Brokki, which was crafted from the skull of Iss.

She starved herself, and sat in meditation for many days. On the twelth day, she was rewarded with glimpse of the mind of Iss, and a vision of the future. Since that time, she has refused to speak of what she saw, saying only that their is a great darkness in the future from which it is impossible to escape.
