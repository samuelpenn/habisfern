Interests
Walgi is an animal goddess, particularly of predators. Her prey is mankind, and she is the defender of animals too wak to defend themselves.
Worshippers
A few hunters follow Walgi. Even more pay their respects to her, and very few choose not to pay attention to her codes.
Stories
There are few stories involving Walgi.

Walgi, Wolf Mother

Walgi is the mother of wolves and other wild animals who can hunt man. She guards the wild forests, seeking out human hunters who don't pay enough respect to those that they hunt.
