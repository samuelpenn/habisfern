Interests
Besenreth is the Twilight Goddess, who lives in the shadowed and hidden places. She is the saviour of the rangers of Tuffin Wood, an independent group of people who live in the thick woods between the borders of the Kingdom of Rorn, and Cadwold.
Worshippers
Only the people of Tuffin Wood worship here in any great numbers, and they do so almost singularly.

Besenreth, Fenya's Shadow

It is said by some that when Fenya was born, she had a sister who was disliked by the gods, and was cast out from their realm. She hid in the dark places of the world, and came to know how to move and live on the borders of society.

After Rorn was slain, and Fenya took up the guiding of the Moon on its path around the world, so Besenreth came to live in the shadow of her sister. She would come out at night, keeping to the shadows cast by the Moon so that none would see her.
