Interests
Bis is god of the sun. He guides the sun in its course around the world in his fiery chariot. He is god of fire, forges, passion and chaos. He is mischivious, and sometimes dangerous.
Worshippers
Bis is venerated for bringing summer to the world. There are small groups of sun cultists who worship him. They use great bonfires as their holy places.

Bis, the Sun God

God of the sun, mischief and chaos. He drives the chariot which pulls the sun around the world once a day, but sometimes strays from his path.
