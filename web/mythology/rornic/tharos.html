Interests
Tharos is the god of murder, individuality, dishonour and selfishness.
Worshippers
No-one admits to following Tharos. Some follow his ways because they want to live their own life, with reliance on no-one else. A few are cultists dedicated to his more violent aspects.
Stories
There are few stories involving Tharos.

Tharos, Night Stalker

Tharos is an Eastling god, who came to the people along with Asabeth. He is a loner, though unlike Meam he is a civilised loner.
