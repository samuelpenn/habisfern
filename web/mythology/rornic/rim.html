Interests
Rim is guardian of the souls of the dead. He gathers the souls to him in the deep realms of the underworld, and guides the souls on to their reincarnation when they are ready.
Worshippers
No one worships Rim, but all ask for his aid to help loved ones find their way in the afterworld.
