Interests
Neya is the Oath Keeper, who never breaks her promises. She is the goddess of marriage, of treaties, of merchants and of peace.
Worshippers
Anyone who makes an oath swears it by the name of Neya. The Queen of Rorn takes Neya as her patron, and her priesthood in turn protects the royal family.

Neya, the Keeper of Oaths

Neya is the goddess of peace, serenity and marriage. She is the Keeper of Oaths, and the vengeance which strikes down those that break oaths made on her name is the only time she shows anger or violence. Her priestesses look after the Queen of Rorn, and decide the succession.
