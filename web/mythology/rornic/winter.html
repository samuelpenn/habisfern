The Age of Winter

A great citadel of ice is built above the ruins of the city of Ubra, and within it dwells Snaw, son of Brokki and leader of the Giants who have raided south with the Ice.

In the south, a man is born, fathered by Haltan on the mortal woman Enrith. He is named Erol, and is taught the ways of warfare by his father, and the ways of magic by his mother. He is a great hero, and wins many battles against the Giants.

He falls in love with a woman named Hreth.

Hreth betrays him at a great battle, and leaves evidence pointing to his mother. It is only after he has killed Enrith for her apparent treachery that he learns the truth, but Hreth has already fled north to Snaw's citadel. So Erol fathers together a group of warriors to go after her.

Erol and most of his companions are slain, before they can bring Hreth to justice. The manage to free Pel though, and discover that Hreth is his daughter, by Gruaga.

The Fall of Ideria

Ideria is the last of the great cities of Man, still beautiful after the other great cities have fallen. It is watched over by Rorn, who is set to guarding the northern reaches against the Ice.
