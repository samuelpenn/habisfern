Interests
Thot is a messenger, but also a spy and god of mischief. He has the ability to make things easier for everyone, but often witholds important information whilst seeming to be helping freely.
Worshippers
Thot is taken as patron by scouts, some Bards, and sometimes those who work in service of a lord, in honour of Thot's position as a messenger of Haltan.
Stories
What stories involve this god?

Thot, the Raven

Created at the dawn of the world, Thot is often the messenger of Haltan. Thot sees and hears everything in the world, and spent the Dawn in the skies above the new creation.
