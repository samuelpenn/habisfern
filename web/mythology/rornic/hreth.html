Interests
Hreth is the goddess of Winter. She is the closest to the Ice of any of the gods.
Worshippers
No-one worships Hreth openely, though a sacrifice is made to her during Winter, in the hope that she will take the sacrifice and give up her rule of the land.

Hreth, Winter's Cloak

She is the goddess of winter, the cold wind and the bringer of snow. Generally considered too close to the Ice to be friendly, she is called upon more to stay away than anything. Her form is that of a great winged beast who can cover the world with her snowy wings.
