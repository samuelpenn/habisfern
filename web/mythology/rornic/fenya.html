Interests
Fenya is goddess of the Moon. Save for Pel, she is said to be the most beautiful of the gods, and she is often associated with love and beauty. She is a hunter also, and enemy of Walgi.
Worshippers
Followers of Fenya come from all walks of life. Many who seek luck in love make offerings to her. It is this aspect which she is venerated for the most.

Fenya, Moon Daughter

She is the goddess who took up the task of leading the moon in its course around the world after the death of Rim. She is goddess of the moon, beauty and the night. She is the brother of Bis, and the daughter of Haltan and Asabeth. She is also seen as the huntress.
