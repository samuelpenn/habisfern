Interests
Estror is the goddess of new beginnings, of birth, spring and dawn. She is the radiant optimist, heralding the start of the day after the cold darkness of night.
Worshippers
Farmers and new mothers venerate Estror, for is for through her that new born babies survive, and crops survive to grow ready for a healthy harvest.

Estror, Goddess of the Dawn

Estror was one of the first gods, created by Rim and Yagma at the dawn of the world. She takes after Yagma the most, taking some of her responsibility onto herself.

After Yagma and Rim had breathed life into the world, Estror went out amongst the new life, and tended to it, helping it grow into what it is today. She tended grass and bushes and trees, the fish in the sea and the animals on the land. She has a love for all things that grow, and is saddened by their deaths at the end of their life.

Though Estror and Yagma are venerated for much the same things, they have a different outlook. Estror truly loves all that lives, whilst Yagma is uncaring and impassionate. Yagma is as much a goddess of death as she is of life.
