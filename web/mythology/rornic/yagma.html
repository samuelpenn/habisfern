Interests
Yagma is the goddess of life and death. Impassionate and uncaring, she creates all, but also destroys it. She is the cycle of nature, of survival, life, and ultimately the end of things.
Worshippers
She is primarily worshipped as a fertility goddess.

Yagma, Earth Mother

Yagma and Rim are the creators of the world, born out of the dreams of Iss before the dawn of time. She symbolises the wheel of life, from conception, through birth and life, to death.
