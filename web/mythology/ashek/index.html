The One God
Followers of the Cult believe that Ashek is the only god, and all other gods are just one reflection of his disjoint personality. They don't have a problem with people worshipping other gods - they just believe that their way is the most efficient.

Cult of Ashek

Ashek is the Mad God, the Insane Creator, who is the force behind the entire universe. Ashek has many fragmented personalities, which others worship as gods in their own right. The 'True' Ashek though is the one taught by the cult.

Cult members believe that the only way to true understanding of Ashek is through insanity. For this reason, they embrace the unstructured, dangerous paths of magic which unhinge the mind, in an attempt to get closer to their god.
